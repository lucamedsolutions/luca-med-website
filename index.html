About Luca Med

Luca Med helps pharmacies deliver medical orders safely and on-time. We combine a vetted driver network, flexible pickup windows, and cold-chain management to protect product integrity and patient outcomes.

From one-off deliveries to recurring schedules, we streamline logistics so your team can focus on care, not couriers.

How It Works

  1. 1

    Pharmacies Create Orders

    Enter order details, recipient info, and any temperature or handling requirements through the dashboard or API.

  2. 2

    On-Demand Pickup Is Scheduled

    Choose pickup windows that fit your workflow—immediate, same-day, or scheduled recurring pickups.

  3. 3

    Delivery Is Executed

    Vetted drivers complete delivery with live tracking, chain-of-custody capture, and proof of delivery.
