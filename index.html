About Us

Who is Luca Med

Luca Med helps pharmacies deliver medical orders safely and on-time. We combine a vetted driver network, flexible pickup windows, and cold-chain management to protect product integrity and patient outcomes.

From one-off deliveries to recurring schedules, we streamline logistics so your team can focus on care, not couriers.

Process

How Luca Med Works

1. Pharmacies Create Orders

Pharmacies submit prescriptions or order requests through the platform — include patient details, pickup window, and special handling notes.

2. On‑Demand Pickup Is Scheduled

Available drivers are matched and a pickup window is scheduled. Drivers receive routing and temperature requirements when applicable.

3. Delivery Is Executed

Driver completes the delivery with proof-of-delivery options and real-time tracking — cold‑chain items handled with required controls.
